LASAGNA SOUP

Description

Perfect meal for cold winter evenings with your significant ones.

If you like pasta and regular lasagna, you will fall in love with

this creamy and tasty soup. It is so easy and affordable dish to make.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna sheets
  • Minced beef
  • Marinara sauce
  • Cream cheese

Steps

  1. Start off by cooking the minced beef
  2. Once the beef is cooked, add spices and the sauce
  3. When the sauce is boiling, add the lasagna sheets
  4. Once the pasta is al dente add the cheese
  5. Taste and add spices if necessary
  6. Enjoy!
