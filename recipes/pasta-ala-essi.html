Spaghetti with creamy minced beef sauce

Description

This creamy version of the traditional spaghetti bolognese

will hit your taste nerves like trying snus for the first time.

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti pasta
  • Minced beef
  • Tomato paste
  • Double cream
  • Onion

Steps

  1. Put spaghetti in boiling water
  2. Cook your minced beef
  3. Once beef is cooked, add spices, cream and the paste
  4. Once spaghetti is cooked, add some pasta boiling water to the sauce
  5. Taste and add spices if necessary
  6. Plate it and enjoy!
