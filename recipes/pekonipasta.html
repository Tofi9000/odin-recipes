BACON PASTA

Description

A delicious and creamy bacon pasta that fulfills your pasta cravings.

This pasta dish will leave your children begging for more.

Ingredients

  • Pasta of your choice
  • High quality bacon
  • Heavy cream
  • grated parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Put the pasta in boiling water
  2. Cut your bacon in small bits
  3. Cook the bacon and add the cream
  4. Add spices and little bit of pasta boiling water
  5. Grate the parmesan and add it to the sauce
  6. Taste and add spices or cheese to match it your tastebuds
  7. Plate it and enjoy!
